Why Drones Make Your Event Memorable: Client Stories from Sri Lanka

Drones have revolutionized how events—from grand public spectacles to intimate weddings—are captured and remembered. By delivering cinematic aerial perspectives, they create shareable moments that resonate long after the occasion ends. The following case studies showcase how Sri Lankan clients have leveraged drone technology to elevate their events and achieve remarkable engagement.

Case Study 1: Dialog Axiata’s “The Future.Today” New Year Drone Countdown

Dialog Axiata PLC broke new ground on 31 December 2024 with Sri Lanka’s first-ever New Year countdown drone show at Galle Face Green.

  • 400 drones choreographed in twelve formations narrated Dialog’s brand story overhead.
  • The spectacle drew over 150,000 live attendees and engaged more than 12 million viewers nationwide, organically generating hundreds of thousands of user-created posts across social media platforms.
  • The campaign reinforced Dialog’s image as a digital innovator and delivered unprecedented brand visibility, with positive sentiment soaring on television, online, and in community forums.
Lasantha Theverapperuma, Group CMO, noted that the show “created a moment of unity and joy” while positioning Dialog at the forefront of technological experiences[1][2].

Case Study 2: Daraz’s First Drone Light Show for Logo Unveiling

In January 2022, e-commerce leader Daraz enlisted Eventistry to choreograph a drone light show unveiling its new logo over Colombo’s Galle Face Green.

  • 150 drones launched from the nearby Port City in two back-to-back eight-minute performances.
  • Over 60,000 spectators witnessed the synchronized aerial choreography, which was accompanied by ground-level festivities and fireworks.
  • Extensive media coverage and social media buzz translated into significant PR value, amplifying Daraz’s rebranding efforts and delivering exceptional earned media metrics.
Eventistry reports that the drone display “attracted multiple media outlets” and yielded an “astounding PR and social media value” for Daraz[5].

Case Study 3: InnovateX 500-Drone Half-Time Show at LPL 2024 Finals

The Lanka Premier League’s 2024 finals at R. Premadasa Stadium featured Sri Lanka’s largest drone performance to date.

  • InnovateX, in partnership with BotLab Dynamics, deployed 500 drones to create live formations illustrating cricket legends’ signature shots, team logos, and the LPL trophy.
  • The show captivated 30,000 in-stadium fans and 220 delegates from 108 ICC member nations, framing the event as both a sporting and technological showcase.
  • By integrating real-time laser effects and synchronized audio cues, the performance set a new benchmark for in-game entertainment and generated widespread acclaim from both local and international audiences[4].

Across these diverse scenarios—public countdowns, corporate rebrands, sports spectacles, and personal milestones—drone videography in Sri Lanka has consistently delivered elevated storytelling, broader audience engagement, and enduring memories. By harnessing the power of aerial cinematography, brands and individuals alike are transforming events into immersive experiences that linger in hearts and feeds long after the final drone lights dim.
